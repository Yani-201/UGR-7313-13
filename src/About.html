About Us

A few words about dream mall...

Dream Mall is a five-story vertical shopping and entertainment center located in the heart of Bole, Addis Ababa in the thriving Country Ethiopia. Shop at the City Target on the second level and visit a 16-screen wide Theater with the largest IMAX in East Africa. Bring your kids, family, friends, anyone because there is something for everyone.
